The App

What is Adobook?

Adobook is a digital recipe book that allows you and your family to share recipes. At Team Flan we hope to keep Filipino traditions alive and preserve culture through food and community.

The app is specifically designed for immigrant Filipino seniors and their family who have a passion for and background in cooking.

02

Features

What Does it Do?

Sharing with family, viewing family recipes, and adding your own recipes are three carefully chosen features of Adobook. The focus on these few key interactions keep the app simple and suitable for users of our domain.

ADD IT

New recipes can be added by scanning physical copies, manually typing information, or by submitting a link to an online recipe

The scanning feature provides the most efficient method of digitizing paper recipe copies

TRANSLATE IT

Recipes can be translated inside of Adobook seemlessly, saving you time in the kitchen when it matters most.

SHARE IT

Adobook lets you stay in touch with family by easily showing you what your family members are cooking and adding to the family recipe book.
