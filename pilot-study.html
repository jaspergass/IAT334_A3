Adobook

04

Pilot Testing

Quality Guaranteed

Adobook has gone through user testing to ensure quality service. After a total of nine online and in-person user tests, Team Flan has made changes according to the observations made during each users’ sessions.

PROCEDURE

FINDING PARTICIPANTS

Including a Filipino family of three, the participants that were gathered for this usability test belong to our user domain.

TASKS

Participants were tasked with completing a series of interactions correlating to the main features of our prototype.

POST TESTING QUESTIONS

After completing these tasks, users were asked to reflect on and answer questions relating to their experience.

RESULTS

Usability Testing with 9 participants

Ages 20 - 61

Found that interactions didn’t translate well or were incomplete

Found the interface straightforward, making tasks easy to complete

Found the tags feature to be convenient but inconsistent (missing in various parts)

Found the interface to be straightforward and the design/ theme calming/family-friendly

Summary

CONCEPT

Main features and interaction paths of the app are appropriate. Adding recipe options were found convenient and easy to use

AESTHETICS

Positive comments relating to the look and feel of the app.

USER EXPERIENCE

Logical, text, and translation errors in the prototype caused some confusion and were annoying to users.

Bugs in the prototype were hindering the ease of completing tasks and trapping users at times.

ACTIONS

Listening to Feedback

Improvements were made according to the data collected from usability tests. Technical issues in the prototype were solved and additional comments made by participants were taken into account when finalizing the app design.
